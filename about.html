About Me


I began my knitting life at 16 years old. I graduated high school early and was looking for a hobby to keep me busy when I wasn't at work. I taught myself using a booklet from Walmart and some regular old acrylic yarn. This was before youtube so it was a little tricky at first but once I got the hang of it, it took over my life. Crocheting followed very quickly after that and spinning came next. As the years passed, those hobbies were always a big part of my life and my kids lives. My passion for sewing started when my youngest daughter was 6 months old. I wanted to make her the most unique clothes. As she got a little older, I ventured into making clothing for myself. I have always had a hard time finding clothes that fit me correctly at the stores and I despise shopping for them. My hobby quickly grew into a full fledged addiction and soon I had made nearly every piece of my kids and my clothing. I soon started to branch off into bags and found yet another obsession.
